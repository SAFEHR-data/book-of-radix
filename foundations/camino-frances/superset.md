<summary><strong>Admin instructions to reset a password or add a new researcher user.</strong></summary>

The current Superset admins are Steve, Sam, Peien, and Paul.

To reset a user's password (if they've forgotten it).

1. Log in to Superset.
2. In the top-right of the UI select "Settings" > "List Users".
3. Find the entry for the user and click the 🔍 "Show record" icon\
   &#xNAN;_(notably \*not\* the "Edit Record" option which you would have expected)_.
4. At the bottom of the page, under "Audit Info", click "Reset Password".
5. Choose a simple, memorable one-off password for them and tell them privately.
6. The user can then log in.
   * Instruct them to go to "Settings" > User "Info" and change their password to something secret and memorable to them. They can use anything they like. Admins can't see their choice.

To add a new user (a new reseracher wants access to Superset).

Follow 1. and 2. above

3. Click the green "+" icon "Add a new record" to the top right of the table.
4. Fill in their data.

Follow steps 5. and 6. above.
